# Parina Bhardwaj
`Machine Learning` · `NLP` · `Full Stack` · `DSA`

> *building things. breaking things. occasionally both.*

---

I'm a CS student who got hooked on ML and never quite recovered.

My work sits at the crossroads of **Machine Learning, NLP, and Full Stack Development** — I love
the full pipeline, from cleaning messy data and training models to building the interface that makes
the output actually useful to a real person. Recent projects include geospatial crime analysis,
a collaborative filtering movie recommender, and fantasy player predictions powered by real match data.
I also care about clean code, good DSA fundamentals, and building things that solve problems
people actually have — not just impressive demos that fall apart under pressure.

>Currently: Seeking open source opportunities ·Experimenting with AI-driven ideas ·Spending an unreasonable amount of time on problems caused by one missing bracket
